Pizza

Pizza is an Italian dish made of dough with cheese and sauce spread on top

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Vegetables of choice

Steps

  1. Add water to the flour and mix to create a dough
  2. Spread the dough in a circle shape
  3. Add tomato sauce
  4. Add cheese
  5. Add vegetables of choice
  6. Put in oven
